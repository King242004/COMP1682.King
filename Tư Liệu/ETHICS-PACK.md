   information is collected.

  Informed Consent Form

[Tên đầy đủ của mày]

# PHẦN 2 · NỘI DUNG TRẢ LỜI CHO FORM

Form mỗi trường một khác, nhưng gần như luôn hỏi những mục dưới đây. Ta soạn sẵn câu trả lời, mày copy vào đúng ô tương ứng.

### Tiêu đề nghiên cứu
> Design and Evaluation of a Condition Aware AI Nutrition Assistant for Vietnamese Adults Managing Diet Related Chronic Conditions

### Mục đích nghiên cứu
> The research investigates how adults managing diet related chronic conditions make everyday food decisions, and evaluates the usability of a mobile application developed to support those decisions. Data collection has two purposes. The questionnaire identifies user requirements and current pain points. The User Acceptance Testing measures the usability of the developed application.

### Ai tham gia và tuyển bằng cách nào
> Participants are adults aged 18 or over. Questionnaire respondents are recruited through personal networks and through online communities concerned with nutrition and chronic condition management, with the permission of group administrators. User Acceptance Testing participants are recruited from personal networks. Participation is voluntary and unpaid. No vulnerable groups are targeted and no participant under 18 is included.

### Thu thập dữ liệu gì
> The questionnaire collects age band, gender, whether the respondent manages a diet related condition, current dietary tracking habits, and opinions on application features. It does not collect names, contact details, medical records or any directly identifying information.
>
> User Acceptance Testing records task completion observations and System Usability Scale responses. Participants use pre-created test accounts rather than personal accounts, so no personal health data is entered.

### Rủi ro với người tham gia
> The research is low risk. Participants are not subjected to any intervention and receive no medical or dietary advice as part of the study. The questionnaire asks whether a respondent manages a health condition, which is sensitive in nature, so it is answered from a fixed list, may be skipped, and is collected anonymously so that it cannot be linked to an individual.
>
> The application displays a disclaimer stating that it does not replace professional medical advice.

### Đồng ý tham gia
> Informed consent is obtained before participation. Questionnaire respondents read an information statement and must actively confirm consent before any question is presented. User Acceptance Testing participants read the Participant Information Sheet and sign the Informed Consent Form before the session.

### Lưu trữ và bảo mật dữ liệu
> Questionnaire responses are held in the researcher's institutional account and exported to a password protected local folder. Consent forms are stored separately from response data. Participants are referred to in the report as P1, P2 and so on, and are never named. All research data is destroyed after the project has been assessed.

### Quyền rút lui
> Participants may withdraw at any point without giving a reason and without any disadvantage. Because questionnaire responses are anonymous, withdrawal after submission is not possible, and this is stated clearly before consent is given. User Acceptance Testing participants may withdraw during or after the session, in which case their data is deleted.

> 💡 Câu về "withdrawal after submission is not possible" là chi tiết mà **hội đồng đạo đức rất để ý**. Khảo sát ẩn danh thì không rút lại được vì không biết phiếu nào của ai, và bắt buộc phải nói trước điều đó. Nhiều sinh viên quên mục này và bị trả hồ sơ.

# PHẦN 3 · PARTICIPANT INFORMATION SHEET

Đưa cho người tham gia UAT đọc trước khi ký. In ra hoặc gửi file.

---

**PARTICIPANT INFORMATION SHEET**

**Study title:** Design and Evaluation of a Condition Aware AI Nutrition Assistant for Vietnamese Adults Managing Diet Related Chronic Conditions

**Researcher:** `[[Tên mày]]`, BSc Hons Computing, `[[Tên trường]]`
**Supervisor:** `[[Tên supervisor]]`
**Contact:** `[[Email mày]]`

**What is this study about**
This study is part of my final year project. I have developed a mobile application that helps people track what they eat and provides dietary guidance appropriate to a declared health condition. I would like to find out how easy the application is to use.

**Why have I been asked**
You have been invited because you are an adult who may use an application of this kind. You do not need any technical knowledge to take part.

**What will I be asked to do**
The session takes about 20 minutes. You will be asked to complete a small number of everyday tasks in the application, for example recording a meal or viewing your weekly summary. Afterwards you will complete a short questionnaire of 10 statements about how easy the application was to use.

You will use a test account that has been created for the study. You will not be asked to enter your own health information.

**Do I have to take part**
No. Participation is entirely voluntary. You may stop at any time, and you do not have to give a reason. Nothing happens if you decide to stop, and there is no disadvantage to you.

**What happens to my information**
Your responses are stored securely and used only for this academic project. You will not be named in the report. Participants are referred to only as P1, P2 and so on. All study data is deleted once the project has been assessed.

**Are there any risks**
No. You are not being tested and there are no right or wrong answers. I am testing the application, not you. The application does not give medical advice and is not a substitute for consulting a doctor.

**Who can I contact**
If you have any questions before, during or after the session, please contact me at `[[email]]`, or my supervisor at `[[email supervisor]]`.

Thank you for considering taking part.
